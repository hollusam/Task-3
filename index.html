Italy!

If you`re thinking about visiting Italy for a holiday, then here are some things you should know before traveling there: Italy is a country that has so much to offer. The people, the culture, the food, and everything else. Italy is such a beautiful country. It`s on of the favorite places to visit especially for holidays.

*Italy is huge!* You`ll need to do plenty of research before deciding which region you want to visit because they vary greatly in size ans geography.

How to have the best Italy holidays? There are so many areas to explore, from Tuscany and Liguria, with their hills and mountains, to Sicily and Sardinia with their beautiful beaches. We`ve picked out the best Italy holiday destinations so you can get the most out of your trip.

Destinations you can visit

Florence

With a population of 372,038 (metropolitan area: 1.5 million), Florence is the eigth largest city in Italy.

Rome

Do you know that in ancient Rome, only free-born men were allowed to wear togas, a sign of Roman citizenship. Women wore stolas, the female version of togas, made from linen.

Milan

The Milan cathedral, also known as the Duomo di Milano, is a massive church located on Piazza del Duomo, the main square in Milan. It`s the 3rd largest catholic church in Europe
